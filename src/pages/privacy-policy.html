Privacy Policy

Last Update June 2, 2023

Summary of Key Points

This privacy policy disclosure describes what we do with the data we collect from you through our Newsletter and Contact forms.

  • We do not share any of your information with third parties.
  • The information we collect is only used specifically as defined in the sections below.
  • For ticketing, please see EventBrite's Privacy Policy.

Newsletter

When submitting your information to our Newsletter form, we will keep this email address on file to be used when sharing relevant news and announcements about Blissfest, including, but not limited to - lineup changes, policy and FAQ updates, times and scheduling. At anytime you can use our Contact form to unsubscribe from this list.

Contact

Our contact form does not collect any of your information and will not be included on the Newsletter distribution. It is intended for direct communication with the Blissfest organizers to get any help and support you need regarding using the website, purchasing tickets, or questions about the event.
